Current Endeavours

I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Electrical Engineering through dissertation. My research involves implementation of clock recovery over Ethernet. I'm applying audio streaming as a use-case, but the methods developed should be applicable in any scenario for critical timing. The hope is that the framework I develop will enable Ethernet to be used for real-time events such as streaming, which will cut costs in many applications.
I also spend my time working on resources for students learning embedded systems. You can view those recources on my GitHub page (Prac Manual, Lab Handbook). I also create YouTube videos as a means of rapid learning, which are all viewable here.
